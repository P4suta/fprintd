Code in this repository that carries the SPDX identifier `LicenseRef-NBIS-PD` is an
ORIGINAL, clean-room reimplementation of a public-domain NBIS algorithm (currently
the BOZORTH3 fingerprint matcher), written from the stock NBIS specification and
verified black-box against the stock NBIS output. It is isolated in its own crate so
it never contaminates the project's permissive (`MIT OR Apache-2.0`) core, and it is
deliberately NOT derived from libfprint's patched `nbis/` copy (whose modifications
carry libfprint's LGPL-2.1-or-later terms). See ARCHITECTURE.md §Provenance & licensing.
