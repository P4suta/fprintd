# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming, harassment-free place to collaborate, for
everyone — regardless of experience, background, identity, or how they use a
fingerprint reader. We pledge to treat one another with respect and good faith.

## Our standards

Behavior that helps the community:

- Being kind, patient, and welcoming, especially to newcomers.
- Assuming good intent, and offering and accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the people in it.

Behavior that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory remarks, public or private.
- Personal or political attacks, or sustained disruption of discussion.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
  setting.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, and any
official channel — and when an individual is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to
the maintainers through GitHub's private reporting on this repository.
Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially and in good faith. Maintainers
may take any action they deem appropriate, from a private warning to a ban from
project spaces, and are obligated to respect the privacy and safety of reporters.
